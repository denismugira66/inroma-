About Us

Your trusted partner in pest control and cleaning services

Our Story

Inropa Fumigants & Company Ltd was founded in 2010 with a clear mission: to provide safe, effective, and professional pest control and cleaning services to homes and businesses across Kenya.

Over the past 15+ years, we have grown from a small team of passionate technicians to a trusted industry leader, serving thousands of satisfied clients in Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, and beyond.

Our commitment to quality, safety, and customer satisfaction has earned us a reputation as one of Kenya's most reliable service providers in the pest control and cleaning industry.
